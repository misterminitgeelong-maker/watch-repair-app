Game Overview
Islands

4 Islands Total

Glondara → Karyd → Oseongo → Island 4 (final). Each island unlocks new mechanics, monsters, and systems.

Prestige System

Rebirth

Resets Main/Class levels. Formula: LVL^0.8 × time_multiplier. Optimal cadence: ~48h. Free Rebirth Glyph preserves levels.

Core Stats

STR · DEX · INT

Earned from talent points on level-up. Island 2: STR not required early. Island 3: Paladin abilities use STR.

Key Currency

Reagents

Used for almost everything. Farming reagents is the core game loop. Liselda lvl 10 is the key reagent companion.

Global Priorities (All Stages)
  • P1Rebirth Glyph (Free) — Always get this first from the Glyph shop. Preserves Main + Class levels on rebirth.
  • P1Reagent Glyph — Second priority after free rebirth glyph.
  • P2Knowledge Glyph — Third glyph priority.
  • P1Golden Ichor (ER) — Spend order: Ability Slot #1 first → stats/damage → Diamond Packs. Never buy Berry Gain.
  • P2Check Arena every 1–2 days — Each first clear gives +4% damage permanently.
  • P2Rebirth ~every 48h — Or shorter with Free Rebirth Glyph. Longer rebirths grant more RP but hit diminishing returns.
  • P3Specialised Farm days — 24h reagent + 24h knowledge outperforms 48h of both combined.

🔄 Rebirth Guide

Prestige system — resets Main/Class levels in exchange for Rebirth Points (RP)

Core Formula
RP = LVL^0.8 × time_multiplier
Higher main level and longer time both increase RP gained. The time multiplier means waiting too short between rebirths wastes potential.
Optimal cadence: ~48h — With the Free Rebirth Glyph equipped, you keep all Main + Class levels, so rebirthing becomes much lower cost.
24h Reagent + 24h Knowledge > 48h of both combined — Specialise your farm days rather than splitting time evenly.
Glyph Priority
  • 1stFree Rebirth Glyph — Preserves Main + Class levels on rebirth. Game-changing. Get this immediately when available.
  • 2ndReagent Glyph — Boosts reagent income significantly.
  • 3rdKnowledge Glyph — For knowledge-gated progression.
Rebirth Tips
  • TipRebirth every ~2 days early on to build stats. With Free Rebirth Glyph, rebirth more frequently as levels are preserved.
  • TipOn Island 3, RP boosters: Grasping Shade and Pugulist — farm 12–24h (include Glyph).
  • TipFarm Specialisation: 24h dedicated to one resource type beats splitting time. Rotate reagent day / knowledge day.
  • AvoidDon't rebirth too frequently without the Free Rebirth Glyph — you lose class levels which take time to rebuild.

⚔️ Arena

10 fights total — each first clear grants permanent +4% damage bonus (max +40%)

General Rules
Check arena every 1–2 days. Your stats grow continuously — a fight that was impossible last week may now be clearable.
Disabled abilities: Buff/debuff/double-damage/disarm abilities are removed in arena. Preview allowed abilities by clicking "Change Loadout" from within a fight before committing.
In arena: Armor is tripled. HP regeneration is disabled. Can't change loadout once started.
Fight 7 is a major wall — don't expect to go straight from Fight 6 to Fight 7. It requires significantly better stats.

🙏 Worship System

Island 3 mechanic — deities, offerings, blessings, relics, and the Reliquary

Unlock Sequence
  • Step 1Only Oq is available at first — begin levelling Oq's favor
  • Step 2Reach Level 1 favor with Oq → Reliquary and Incense Offerings unlock
  • Step 3Use Incense to unlock a location on the map → other deities + Gold Offerings unlock
  • Step 4Level up another deity with Gold Offerings → Blessings and Oil Offerings unlock
In-game text tells you what to do next — follow the prompts.
Reliquary
12×12 grid — place Relics to generate resources. Random placement works, but careful optimised placement greatly improves yields.
After Arena Fight 8 clear: Receive a free placeable Reliquary gem with +40% boost. Use it wisely.
Blessings
Use Blessing Level 3 for Island 3 content. Path toward knowledge or reagent depending on current task. Respeccing is free — you only need 5 nodes for a full path.
Nai trial becomes manageable around Blessing Tier 2 combined with Island 3 equipment.

Ring Quality Tiers
  • QualityNormal → Superb → Divine — quality increases stats and ascension potential
  • TipSkip Empowerment until Superb quality. Empowering lower quality rings wastes resources.
  • InfoA0 accessories can be empowered fully in ~4 minutes — tedious but effective at low ascension
  • AscendRings can be ascended at level 2+ with sacrifice, or level 5+ without sacrifice
Merge System
Only 1 merge per ring. The merged ring is destroyed. The merged stat adjusts to the main ring's ascension level.
Flexible: You can change the merged ring later by merging a different ring into the main ring (previous merge is lost).
Ascending after merge: When you ascend from L5 → L6, both the main stat and merged stat scale up.
Best Ring Combinations
Peak combos (Ceruhe):
• Lunar / Reagent — best reagent ring setup
• Solar / Faith — best Faith ring setup
Other stats have more flexibility depending on your current bottleneck.
For Arena: Damage/Armor combo — armor triples in arena so armor rings become extremely effective.
Island 4 readiness: Solar/Lunar gen rings at Ascension 2; battle stat rings at Ascension 0.

✨ Faith System

Island 3 mechanic — breaks monster shields faster

What is Faith?
All Island 3+ monsters have a shield. Faith reduces the time it takes to break that shield, directly increasing kill speed and thus all farming rates.
High Faith = faster kills = more reagents, XP, and RP per hour. It's one of the most important Island 3 stats to invest in early.
